tweetbot-thumbnails-on(1) -- turn thumbnails on in Tweetbot
===========================================================

## SYNOPSIS

`tweetbot-thumbnails-on`

## DESCRIPTION

The tweetbot-thumbnails-on(1) command turns thumbnails on in Tweetbot, and restarts the app if it's running.
