textual-thumbnails-off(1) -- turn thumbnails off in Textual 5
=============================================================

## SYNOPSIS

`textual-thumbnails-off`

## DESCRIPTION

The textual-thumbnails-off(1) command turns thumbnails off in Textual 5, and restarts the app if it's running.
